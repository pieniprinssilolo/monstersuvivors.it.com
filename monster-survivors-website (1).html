About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where players must fight against waves of increasingly difficult monsters. With each wave you survive, you'll gain new abilities and power-ups to help you last even longer against the monster horde.

Inspired by popular roguelike survival games, Monster Survivors offers a unique twist on the genre with its distinctive monster designs, diverse character abilities, and engaging progression system that will keep you coming back for more.

Whether you're looking for a quick gaming session or a marathon survival challenge, Monster Survivors adapts to your playstyle, making it perfect for both casual and hardcore gamers.

Tips & Tricks

Focus on Movement

Staying mobile is key to survival. Keep moving to avoid being surrounded by monsters.

Prioritize Upgrades

Choose upgrades that complement your character's strengths or compensate for their weaknesses.

Manage Resources

Health pickups and special abilities are limited - use them strategically when you're in real danger.

Learn Enemy Patterns

Each monster type has unique attack patterns and weaknesses. Learn to recognize them for easier victories.
